What is a Personal Loan?

Personal Loan as the name suggests is a loan take to meet your personal needs which could be anything ranging from buying a gift for your special one, a vacation, some home renovation, marriage, down payment for a house, etc and in unfortunate instances for scenarios like medical emergency or the likes.

Personal Loan options in India

Borrowers who want to avail or get a personal loan can be segregated into three key buckets i.e.

  • Borrowers new to credit (NTC) and have never taken a loan or credit card in the past.
  • Borrowers with a good credit score and repayment track record
  • Borrowers with delayed payments and lower credits score. This segment can be further segregated into people with just a few delays and those with multiple delays and very bad credit score including suits filed against them

Personal Loan via Banks/NBFCs

Some banks cater to new-to-credit customers and start with a smaller loan amount. The key to getting a loan here is a job in an established company, a good salary and a healthy bank balance. The second category are those borrower who every bank would want to give a loan to or in fact a higher loan. However, other criteria like your salary, your debt to income ratio, credit card outstanding, house type, etc will also play a very critical role. The chance of a borrower with a loan Cibil score and multiple delays getting a loan is very bleak. However, a borrower with a low Cibil score and a few delays can still salvage the situation by ensure proper repayment of existing line of credit. There are a few banks and NBFCs which help in improving the credit score by various means such as a secured line of credit and more. Multiple banks and NBFCs are continuously fighting for market share to ensure better revenues and an asset class to leverage upon.
